# Friends of Presta: The PrestaShop Expert Directory and Open Source E-commerce Community

**Reading time: 5 min**
**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Friends of Presta: What Is It?

**Friends of Presta** is a [French non-profit association](https://friendsofpresta.org/) founded in **2021**. It brings together **agencies**, **freelancers**, and **module publishers** specializing in PrestaShop e-commerce — in France and across Europe.

In just a few years, it has become the **reference independent body** for the PrestaShop ecosystem. Its three core missions:

- **Directory** — List certified professionals (agencies, freelancers, publishers)
- **Events** — Organize community gatherings (FOP Days → EO2S)
- **Security** — Run the PrestaShop [Hall of Fame security](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/hall-of-fame)

The community has **1,000+ members** on its Slack workspace and continues to grow.

---

## The Directory: Finding a PrestaShop Expert

The heart of the association is its **[expert directory](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/directory)**. Three types of profiles:

| Profile | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| **Agencies** | Web agencies specializing in PrestaShop, migration, redesign |
| **Freelancers** | Developers, integrators, PHP/Symfony consultants |
| **Publishers** | PrestaShop module creators (payments, SEO, logistics…) |

Each provider is reviewed by the community — giving **rare transparency** in the ecosystem.

> 💡 If you're looking for a PrestaShop provider, this is the first place to check before marketplaces or forums.

---

## What Sets Friends of Presta Apart

**Independence.** Friends of Presta is **not tied to PrestaShop SA**. It's a neutral association governed by its members. This means:

- No conflict of interest in recommendations
- Open community governance
- Impartial events (unlike events sponsored by a single vendor)

**Security.** The association runs the [Hall of Fame security](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/hall-of-fame) — a space dedicated to security alerts and patches for the ecosystem.

**Events.** From local meetups to structured conferences (EO2S), Friends of Presta drives the **community calendar** of the ecosystem.

The [friendsofpresta.org](https://friendsofpresta.org/) site organizes its content into **6 sections**:

- **[Modules](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/modules-prestashop)** — Module ecosystem
- **[Projects](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/projets)** — Calls for tender between members
- **[Job Board](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/offres-emploi)** — PrestaShop recruitment
- **[Events](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/evenements)** — Community calendar

---

## Elected Members and Governance

Friends of Presta is led by **[elected members](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/membres-elus)**. Among them: **Christophe Vidal** (President) and **Thomas Rayrat** (Vice President), who regularly co-sign major statements from the association — like the [first-ever French CMS E-commerce Barometer](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/evenements/eo2s-2026).

This associative governance gives the ecosystem an **independent voice** in the face of PrestaShop's evolution and its new owners.

---

## How to Join the Association

Membership is **open to all** professionals in the ecosystem:

1. **Sign up** at [friendsofpresta.org](https://friendsofpresta.org/)
2. **Access Slack** — 1,000+ active members
3. **Attend events** — meetups, EO2S, workshops
4. **Contribute** — security, modules, content, governance

Whether you're a freelance developer, web agency, or merchant with a technical team — there's a place for you.

It's **today** that [EO2S — Ecommerce Open Source Summit](https://friendsofpresta.org/en/evenements/eo2s-2026) takes place at Le Nida, Issy-les-Moulineaux (Paris). The first edition of this European open source e-commerce summit, hosted by Friends of Presta.
